Admin
    *properties
        -name
        -password
        
    *methods
        -approve/decline requests (ws)
        -register students
student
    *properties
        -name  
        -id
        -department
        -batch
    *methods
        -request permissions
wards   
    *properties
        -username
        -password
    *methods
        -get approved requests (ws)
        -change status of permission of students after they leave

superAdmin
    *properties
        -username
        -password
    *methods
        -register admins
        -register students
        -approve/decline
permission
    *properties
        -reason
        -requestedBy (student)
        -status (pending/approved/completed/rejected)
        -approvedBy (admin/superAdmin)
    *methods
        -admins approve permissions
        -students request permissions
